Why it matters: The market is repricing AI execution risk alongside AI capex risk — not just "will companies spend?" but "will they deliver?" Gemini delays erode Alphabet's AI credibility just as Apple (via Gemini) becomes its biggest distribution partner.

OpenAI + Broadcom: 10 Gigawatt AI Accelerator Commitment to 2029 — But IPO May Be Delayed

Why it matters: The AVGO / NVDA / MSFT supply chain thesis remains intact at the infrastructure level, but an OpenAI IPO delay removes a key positive sentiment catalyst that had been priced in by the market.

142,000 Tech Layoffs in 2026 — AI Infrastructure Costs Fund the Cuts
